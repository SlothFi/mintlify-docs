## Prohibited Actions

### Multi-accounting
Users are not allowed to create more than 1 account per device.

### Scams & Social Engineering
Players cannot engage in phishing schemes or trick others into surrendering private keys, resources, or game assets.

### Technical & Non-Technical Exploits
Client hacking, DDoS attacks, double spending, smart contract exploits, and bug abuse intended to gain unfair progression advantages or items beyond earned skill levels are forbidden.

### Bots & Automation
While informational browser extensions are permitted, automation of any kind is not allowed.

### Account Sales
Trading items and monsters is acceptable under game rules, but account transfers are prohibited and result in bans.

<Warning>
We reserve the right to retroactively update this prohibited list and/or take action on behaviors that harm the integrity of the game or community, even if not currently covered here.
</Warning>

## Enforcement

The platform uses an automated **Bot Eye 3.0 system** for enforcement, with bans typically restricting economic and multiplayer access rather than complete removal. Staff also conduct periodic investigations.

## Reporting & Rewards

Players can report exploits, bugs, or rule-breaking behavior to receive rewards scaled by issue severity.

For questions, visit the [Spellborne Discord](https://discord.gg/spellborne).
