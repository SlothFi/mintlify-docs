Spellborne is a retro inspired monster collector RPG that blends nostalgic gameplay elements and modern technology. The game combines mechanics from Pokemon, Tamagotchi, and Stardew Valley, featuring player-controlled markets for creatures, resources, and cosmetics.

## Key Facts

- **Developer:** Mon Studios (independent studio)
- **Platforms:** Browser, iOS, Android — PC and Mac via Epic Games Store coming soon
- **Milestones:** 175K signups, 32M battles and 4M monsters captured within the first year
- **Expansion:** Multiple titles planned including Guardians of Celestia and Spellborne Rogue
