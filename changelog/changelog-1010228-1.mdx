A minor update focusing on battle system improvements, performance improvements, and testing of multiple core features.

## New Updates
- **PvP Leagues:** The leagues system has seen a few major improvements covering the battle system and the reward structure.

*Randoms League:*
Players get access to pre-defined squads of monsters based on the most used squads in PvP Leagues till current date. This allows new players to get a jist of how PvP Leagues work, what monsters are according to their play-style and more, without having to grind towards creating random squads and testing them out.

*Elo Updates:*
Starting PvP Leagues Era 4, the ELO earned from bot battles is based on the current league of the player, instead of the default value of 2.

Bronze: 16 
Silver: 12 
Gold: 8 
Platinum: 4 
Diamond: 2 
Conqueror: 1
- **Battle System: **The battle system has seen a user interface update that focuses on restructuring the interface to better support multi-monster battles, including co-op, raids and monster hordes.

While this interface update is not final, it is a step towards adding support for the upcoming features mentioned above.
- **Twitch Drops: **The first batch of twitch drops for Spellborne is live until 12th Feb 2025. Players can earn in-game items by supporting their favourite Spellborne content creators on twitch.
- Added mutations for 1 monster (Baby Kong).
- Added infrastructure updates to support NFT monsters, this feature is currently being tested in the development build and may not be fully live in production yet.
- Added infrastructure updates to support monster and item bazaar, this feature is currently being tested in the development build and may not be fully live in production yet.
- Improved loading speeds for map-to-map switch, with an upcoming update that will focus on reducing the loading time between maps to almost instant.
