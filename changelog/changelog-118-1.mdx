A minor update focusing on winter content update, guilds base release, and a few other feature updates.

## New Updates
- **Winter Season**: The winter season is here, this brings an update to the whole overworld map, including 3 new monsters, winter-only crops and more. Winter Season will stay active until 25th March 2025.
- **Spellborne Wrapped**: Airdrop claim for S1 players is live, players can claim their token rewards off-chain.
- **After-Market Sales**: Ability to sell certain items back to the after-market for gold nuggets or compost.
- **Wheel of Fortune V2**: A new version of the Wheel of Fortune is live with bigger and exciting rewards.
- **Guilds V1**: Includes Fortunes of War, Guild Chest, and foundational guild features. Might be a bit buggy, expect fixes in future updates.
- **Verified Creators**: Support your favourite creators! Use their codes to make purchases, with 10% of the amount going directly to them (subject to program requirements).
- **Day-Care Improvements**: New Innate Min-Max filters and bug fixes for preferences.
- **Chat Links**: You can now showcase your monsters, items, and skins to other players in the chat.
- **Apartment Renting for BORNE**: Rent apartments from other players to create guilds or host custom tournaments. This feature will remain off-chain until TGE.
- **Improved User Interfaces**: Updates across various sections of the game.
- **Bug Fixes**: Multiple fixes for moves, abilities, Bottle Caps quest, and the Double XP Week event.

## Known Bugs
- Certain monster abilities might not work correctly.
- Certain monster breeds might not be available as followers.
- PvP leaderboard might be missing some data.
- Guild User interfaces might be buggy.
- Claims for NFT rewards might be buggy.
