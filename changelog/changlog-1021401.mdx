A minor update focusing on staking, monster mounts, and battle pass.

## New Updates
- Monster Mounts: Stage 3 mutations for Skitten, Presleaf and Caiman are now live in-game along with the ability to mount them. Additional mutation for Leyang added as well.
- PvP Leagues: Infinity Update is live, infinite rewards, infinite fun.
- Tomato Tomato: Stellar pass is here, with exciting new skins, items and rewards. Available till 8th October, 2025.
- Settlers Glory: Token Staking is live, stake your BORNE tokens to earn glory points over time, stake early for boosted multipliers.
- Treasures of Celestia: Gizmo has been added to the rewards list, make sure to win one before it disappears.
- Emotes are now live in the battle system, with custom key bindings for battle UI.
- Bug fixes for battle system, improved connection and phased animations for user disconnects.
- Performance updates for users using iOS and Android builds.
