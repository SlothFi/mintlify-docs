This is a minor update focusing on system upgrades, bug fixes and new content.

## New Updates
- New monsters and mutations are here, added 1 new monster, and monsters such as Bengu, Lektocute, Potamin and more get access to new mutations.
- A spooky Copybara has been roaming the dirt roads of Sunshine Trail, available only until 30th November 2024.
- Stellar Pass: Back in Time is live!
- 3 new emojis available in the tools store at Starz Clothing!
- 2 new side quests have been added.

## Bug Fixes/Minor Fixes
- Bug fixes for Forgotten friendships quest.
- Battle Backgrounds now follow the season flavours.
- Reworked certain monster spawns to fix level growth.
- Bug fixes for certain NPCs not spawning correctly.

## Coming Next
- Boosted EXP Week starts on 8th November, 2024.
- PvP Leagues are under development, should be live mid November, 2024.
- Guilds: Base Systems are under development, should be live end of November, 2024.
- Settlers Glory: Apartment Staking should go live end of November, 2024.

## Ends Soon
- One Step Ahead bundle will end on 10th November 2024 00:00 GMT.
- Summer Skins will end on 15th November 2024 00:00 GMT.

## Notes

### PvP leagues are coming!

The PvP league system will enter into testing towards the end of Season 1. Players will be able to participate in the league systems to get a double innate boosted Woodot as a reward for helping improve the game.

Please look out for future communications related to this.

Happy hunting!
