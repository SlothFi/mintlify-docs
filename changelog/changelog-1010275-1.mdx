A minor update focusing on hordes, battle system fixes, test builds for mining, fishing and settlers glory.

**New Updates**
- **Monster Hordes: **Players can expect wild hordes of multiple monsters to spawn in the battle field. The number may wary anywhere between 2 and 5. This feature is currently in beta phase and will serve as the baseline for multiple features in the future such as raids and 2v2 ranked leagues.
- **Battle System: **This update consists of multiple updates for the battle system including user interface and improvements related to bug fixes and nerfs for moves and abilities.

The battle system now sports a much newer user interface, which helps us support in-game dialogue (with NPCs), multiple enemies in battle field and increases overall readability with a newer font.

- 2 new mutations added (Malvyris and Vaporeel).
- New death animation added for wild monsters.
- Leaden ability (Buff fixed, boost changed from 50% to 25%)
- Bug fixes for moves supporting recursive behaviour.
- Rough Charge (-10 Skill Power, +3 SP cost)
- Flame Armor (+15 SP cost)
- **PvP Leagues: **The 6th era of PvP leagues goes live on March 14, 2025, 14:30 GMT. This update brings a much needed infrastructure update allowing players to re-connect into a PvP battle for much longer and having upto 3 failed tries, resulting in a much more stable experience.
- Stellar Pass: Carnival Days is live!
- New quest "Sailor Moon" added!
- Gem colors have been updated to be consistent with the crystal they're used to craft.
- Multiple character skins and skateboard skins have been fixed.
- Wallet infrastructure update has been completed to be able to support fiat payments in the game.
- Fixed multiple bugs related to monster breeding.
- Fixed multiple bugs related to taskboard.
- Joystick on mobile has been improved to support diagonal movements.
