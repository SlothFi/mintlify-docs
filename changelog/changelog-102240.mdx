A minor update focusing on Guilds V2, interaction bug fixes and preparing for open markets.

## New Updates
- Stellar Pass: Cinco de Mayo is here, with a longer duration of 45 days and 30 more levels.
- PvP Leagues Era 10 is live.
- Guild V2 is a major update improving performance as well as making the guilds system ready for future updates:
- The user interface has been fully redesigned from the ground up.
- Guilds can now upload logos.
- Announcements allow guild leaders to effectively communicate with members.
- Members list has been improved to show last online, as well as contribution points for all members, allowing guild leaders to manage the guild more effectively.
- Guild Donations are here, members can donate gold nuggets to fellow guild members, and earn contribution points.
- Introducing Contribution Points: These points can be collected from Guild Donations, Guild Wars events and more. While there is no utility for these points, Guild Leaders may use them to measure the contribution of various members towards the guild, and reward them accordingly with upcoming Guild tokens.
- Battle System: This update includes multiple fixes for moves and abilities:
- Haunting Vitality (Bug fix)
- Sacrifice (Bug fix)
- Eclipse Resistance (Bug fix)
- Mystic Doll (Bug fix)
- Fixed multiple bugs related to maps such as Hospital, Fabled mountain and Library, as well as reduced lagging issues.
- Fixed bug related to monster followers.
- Fixed bug related to live spectating matches.
- Fixed bug with Sailor Moon quest.
- Players can now directly tap on other players to open interaction menu.
- Soul Exchange V2 has ended. Remaining Soul items can be used in future soul exchange events.

## Known Bugs
- Certain monster abilities might not work correctly.
- Certain monster breeds might not be available as followers.
