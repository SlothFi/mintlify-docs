A minor update focusing on skills, new enchanted monster, bug fixes and improvements for day care v2.

## New Updates
- **Daycare: **Final few updates have been added to the daycare system, including:
- Support for custom tags.
- Faster queries (Upto 50%)
- Separate filters for eggs only search.
- **Enchanted Monster: **A new enchanted monster can be found in the wild, will you be the one to catch it first?
- **Skills: **The previous character attributes (charisma, intelligence, luck) are being replaced with a new skills system, to better emphasise interaction with the game world and your monsters.

Skills are built around a particular kind of activity you may spend a lot of time doing in game, and are the new way that various things are gradually unlocked. Each skill can be levelled up by gaining experience points in that skill, through performing relevant actions associated with different parts of the game.

The current max level for each skill is 10, though this will gradually be raised with future updates.

To allow you to further customise your character and differentiate yourself from your fellow adventurers, every skill line comes with a set of "Specialisms", which are permanent bonuses related to that skill that you can select one of every five levels.

**Training**
Exp source: When your monsters gain exp during wild battles.
Per level bonus: Increased monster catch rate when using crystals.

**Farming**
Exp source: Harvesting crops. (Crops that take longer to grow give more exp.)
Per level bonus: Unlocks more seedpack types.

**Crafting**
Exp source: Crafting items. (Recipes that require more ingredients give more exp.)
Per level bonus: Unlocks more crafting recipes.

**Breeding**
Exp source: Breeding monsters.
Per level bonus: Eggs hatch faster.

Fishing and mining will be added in a future update.
- **Powerups: **Powerups are limited time/move based boosts that can be assigned to a player's profile. These boosts can improve yield from gold nugget drops, more experience per win, more visible points or even let a player see innate stats of wild monsters for a limited period of time.

The first powerup is accessible via "Eye of Athena" item, allows players to view innate stats of wild monsters for 5 minutes when active.
- **New Items: **EXP Share and Potential booster items, namely Forced Experience Device and Forced Potential Device have been added to the game.
- **Wheel of Fortune: **3 new items have been added to the wheel of fortune for a limited time. Players can now also exchange mirror shards for silver tickets in after market.
- **User Interface: **Countless improvements have been made to the user interface for mobile, bringing the game in-sync plus add support for Game Mode on iOS.
- **Login Improvements: **Loading time during login has been reduced from 8 seconds to 3 seconds in most cases, with major portion of the game feeling considerably faster.
