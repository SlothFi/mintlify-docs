## Notes

### Season 1 Ends Soon!

The first season of Spellborne ends this November. Players will receive an in-game notification regarding the exact date one week in advance.

The user points collected during Season 1 will undergo a system verification to remove all bots and cheaters, thus players may see changes in their rankings. The points will then be converted to a rank based token distribution during the token generation event.

Further, the total airdrop for Season 1 has been extended to 12% which covers F2P players, Apartment and Monster NFT Holders.

### Future Seasons

Starting with the second season, the gameplay points distribution will rely on PvP leagues Eras.

An era is a fixed duration of 2 months for PvP leagues, at the end of which a snapshot is taken, player ELOs are reset and those points get added to the seasonal airdrop.

Thus, players get a concrete idea of timeline as well as their airdrop distribution is not over-claimed by players coming in at a later stage.

### Moving On-chain

The game will move to fully on-chain gameplay and data management with multiple feature releases.

**Apartment Staking**: Starting with apartment staking, where apartment holders can stake their apartments to receive glory points. In-addition to other features such as Renting APIs.

**Game Marketplaces**: Following staking, the game will introduce in-game marketplaces for players to be able to trade monsters, more information related to this will be shared in a separate update.

**Token**: Once the marketplaces are live, and the game economy is mature, the token generation event will take place with all the past airdrop claims for Season 1 and Season 2 being realised.

**The token generation event may take place at any point in Season 2**. (*Communication related to this will start in advance giving players an idea of the exact date once it is final*.)
