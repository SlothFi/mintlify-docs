A minor update focusing on hordes, mining, and improvements for the battle system.

**New Updates**
- **Monster Hordes:** User interface, as well as positioning for hordes has received a major update, moving this feature out of alpha into beta release. Players will encounter hordes upto 5 monsters from now on in wild battles.
- **Mining: **Mining Skill is live. Players can mine rocks in the Fabled Mountain (Unlocks after Everything is Makeshift quest), to get items such as minerals, gems and even gold nuggets.

The rocks that players encounter spawn randomly every 24 hours. This feature is meant to be considered in alpha phase and may see major changes or improvements.
- **Farming: **Fertilisers are here! Players can now craft three different types of fertilisers using minerals (found from mining) and compost. These fertilisers can be stacked on top of each other, and affect different things such as reducing growth time, increasing produce or increasing time before a crop goes bad.
- **Battle System: **The following bug fixes have been added:
- Regeneration (Fixed to exclude fainted monsters)
- Trunkblade (Move fixed)
- Herbivore (Ability fixed)
- Elcabra stats rework
- Malvyris moveset rework
- ThickArmor (Damage reduced to 33%)
- Burning Spit (Move fixed)
- Fire Spark (Move fixed)
- Swarm Force (Move fixed)
- Mobile Builds for iOS and Android have reached Release Candidate status, with full public release coming soon.
- Multiple monster sprites have been updated.
