## Notes

### Season 1 ends this week!

The first season of Spellborne is coming to a close, with a snapshot of the points system taken on 16th November 2024. After this snapshot, all points will undergo system verification to filter out bots or accounts attempting to exploit the system. Once verified, the final points will be displayed on the in-game dashboard later this month.

**Rewards for the Wheel of Fortune** event will be distributed in the first week of December 2024, following a thorough verification of user accounts to ensure fair play and compliance with referral guidelines.

During this process, players are invited to dive into **Season 2**—launching later this month after the PvP Leagues Test Phase concludes. Season 2 will bring a more competitive edge, offering increased in-game rewards and a token airdrop from the community token pool.

**To recap:**

- **A total 12% of the BORNE token supply has been allocated towards a mix of Apartment NFT Holders, Monster NFT Holders, and Season 1 players.**
- **This 12% supply is a part of a total 50% of BORNE token supply that has been allocated towards Season 1 and future seasons combined.**

**The allocation among Season 1 players will be heavily rank-based, prioritizing the top 5,000 players to ensure they receive a minimum airdrop. An additional portion of the airdrop will be raffled among the remaining players, giving everyone a chance to participate in the rewards distribution.**

**The airdrop will be distributed among players at the BORNE token generation event during Season 2.**

**More details regarding allocations will be shared later this week via our socials.**

*No snapshot has yet been taken for NFT holders or Players.*

### PvP leagues are coming!

The **PvP Leagues Era System** will kick off with a **Test Phase starting on November 16, 2024**, running for one week. During this testing phase, player ELO will not contribute to Season 2 points. However, participants have the chance to earn an exclusive **Double Innate Woodot** as a reward for joining in!

*Hint: Double innate Woodot refers to a Woodot with 2 innate stats of your choice scoring more than 50/63 and gender of your choice.*

If everything in the test phase goes well, then Season 2 will begin on a tentative date of 23rd November 2024 (Subject to change, based on Test Phase performance).
