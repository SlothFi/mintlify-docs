A minor update focusing on battle system updates, system upgrades and more.

**New Updates**
- **Battle System: **The battle system has received few major changes to make the game more fun to play, as well as improvements to make PvP better.

The experience system has been vastly improved to provide higher amount of experience more in line with the level differences and amount of effort spent.

- 2 new mutations added (Wilburp and Sailmost)
- Detoraton and Grenadent nerf (-10 sDEF)
- Pagyong nerf (-10 BST) [-5 HP,-5 ATK, -5 DEF, +5 sATK, -10 sDEF, +10 SPE]
- Nectus buff (+5 ATK)
- Horus buff (+5 HP)
- Wurmaut buff (+10 BST) [+10 DEF, -10 sATK, +10 sDEF]
- Deltabug buff (+20 BST) [+10 ATK, +10 SPE]
- Protection moves (Flame Armor and Protection) cost increased by 10
- Electrical Outlet (Damage fixed)
- Leaden ability (Buff fixed)
- With Love, from Celena bundle is now live, including 1 Golden Ticket for every purchase!
- Joystick on mobile has been improved to have walking and skating speeds consistent with when arrow keys are used.
- Mobile build size reduced by 10%, along with multiple performance improvements.
- Improved flow for in-game payments, allowing players to spend BORNE or staked BORNE for bundles and stellar pass purchases in the future.
- Bug fixed related to Hatch-a-bee quest.
- Bug fixed related to Creator Codes receiving random warnings.
- Character skins are now handled server side, allowing for a lower bundle size, faster loading as well as on-the-go updates for new character skin releases.
