A minor update focusing on Spring season, Tower of Eternity and Global mobile apps release.

**New Updates**
- **Spring Season** is here! Bringing in a fresh feeling of beautiful landscapes and flowerbeds. 3 new monster breeds have been added - Shrumel, Sphynex and Norigami.
- **Tower of Eternity **levels have been updated to include support for Hordes, with inclusion of 2 new levels.
- **New Items **have been added including Hypno Lolipop, Change of Glasses, 7 Monster cosmetics, 3 Crop enchancers, 3 minerals. Players may see some of these items be added to the Wheel of Fortune in next update.
- Mobile Global Release takes place on 26th March 2025, 12PM EST. The iOS and Android builds will go into early access for public, with improved notifications and user interface fixes.
- **Battle System: **The following bug fixes have been added:
- Ocean Waves (Missing SFX added)
- Scubattack (Missing SFX added)
- Tidal Toxin (Bug fix)
- Poisonous Bath (Bug fix)
- Inventory user-interface has seen improvements to support drag and drop.

## Ends Soon
- PvP Leagues Era 6 ends on 28th March 2025.
