A minor update focusing on new Soul Exchange, bug fixes for guilds, and overall game improvements.

## New Updates
- **Soul Exchange**: The second soul exchange event is live, with 5 new monsters, all designed by the Spellborne community. These 5 monsters will not be available in the wild or after the event ends. Players can collect souls by freeing their monsters from daycare (75% chance), and exchange the souls for these monsters.
- **Wheel of Fortune V2**: The reward structure for Wheel of Fortune has been updated:
- Stellar Passes will appear for claim once every 3 hours.
- Apartments will appear at random times during the day, with max rewards per day limited to 5.
- **Guilds:** The members list view has been updated to support username search, filters and more. Button added for users to be able to jump directly to the guild apartment. More improvements will be added later this week.
- **Bug Fixes: **Bugs have been fixed related to after market sales, kicking members from guilds, guild mail, filters for guild search.
- **PvP Leagues:** The badged leagues has been restructured to incorporate a bigger section of the player-base:
Bronze - 1-500 ELO
Silver - 500-750 ELO
Gold - 750-1250 ELO
Platinum - 1250-2000 ELO
Diamond - 2000-3500 ELO
Conqueror - 3500+ ELO

Additional fixes include increased wait timer for bot battles, automatic ties if both players don't join battle, reduced ELO gain from bot battles and leaderboard ranking.
- **Battle System: **Updated costs for certain moves, abilities and working of statuses such as Poison.
- **Power Ups**: Added structure for power-ups, will fully go live in January 2025.
- **Moves &amp; Abilities Changes:**
Replenish (SP cost increased to 25)
Fresh Waves (SP cost increased to 25)
Royal Growl (SP cost increased to 12)
Water Harvesting (SP cost increased to 30)
Poisonous Bath (SP cost increased to 30)
Lounge (SP cost increased to 50)
Strategic Sleep (SP cost increased to 35)
Explo Roll (Bug fixed related to move target)
Landing (SP cost increased to 50)
Limb Renewal (SP cost increased to 30)
Toxic Shed (Chance to poison enemy set to 50%)
Herbivore [Ability] (Limited to 2 times per battle)
Solid Armour [Ability] (Limited to 3 times per battle)
Poison [Status] (Switched to a buffed version of original poison status, Monster loses 1/32 of its max HP at the first turn of poison. At each turn the damage is increased (2/32, 3/32, 4/32, 5/32, until 6/32). Beyond that, monster loses 6/32 of its maximum HP at each turn)
