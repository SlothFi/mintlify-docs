A minor update focusing on Mondo Swamp, new monsters and future feature updates.

## New Updates
- **Mondo Swamp** has been unlocked, including new monster breeds and a quest rework to fit the lore better.
- Added mutation for Armadrill.
- Added Sacrifice move, shows up for every monster when they are low on stamina.
- Improved design for After Market with key support, making it more compatible with controllers.
- Stellar Pass: 31 days of Christmas is live!
- Fixed bugs related to multiple moves and abilities.

## Known Bugs
- Certain monster abilities might not work correctly.
- Certain monster breeds might not be available as followers.

## Coming Next
- Boosted XP Week starts on 13th December 2024.
- Wheel of Fortune: Golden Ticket, is targeted to go live in December 2024.
- Mobile Builds (iOS and Android) will go into closed testing for holders in December 2024.
- Professions are targeted to go live in December 2024.
- Task Board upgrades are targeted to go live in December 2024.
- Settlers Glory: Apartment Staking, targeted to go live in December, 2024.
- Guilds: Base Systems, targeted to go live in December, 2024.
- Winter Season begins on 29th December 2024.
