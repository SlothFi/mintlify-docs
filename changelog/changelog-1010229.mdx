A minor update focusing on battle system reworks, PvP Leagues Era 4 and more.

## New Updates
- **PvP Leagues: **Era 4 has started, with better ELO rewards from bot battles based on the current league of the player, instead of the default value of 2.

Bronze: 16
Silver: 12
Gold: 8
Platinum: 4
Diamond: 2
Conqueror: 1
- **Follower Cosmetics: **Added support for cosmetics (held items) on follower monsters in overworld. Players will be able to purchase these cosmetics by using Glory points.
- **Battle System: **The battle system has received a few changes to make the game more consistent. Players may test out the game, and share their feedback on our discord server.

- Detoraton and Grenadent nerf (-30 in BST)
- Hemzel and Grimzel boost (+15 BST)
- Flame Armor cost reduced (14 -&gt; 10)
- Inferno modified (Accuracy : 90 -&gt; 85, SP : 40 -&gt; 45)

The damage formula has seen some changes as well:

- Physical and Special moves are now both affected by the type effectiveness, but it's no more x2 but x1.5).
- SameTypeBoost is now a boost of 25% and not 50%
- Damage for higher level monsters (+25) follows a better curve now.

Bug fixes have been made for the following moves:
- Flame Nullifier.
- Rage Skill.
- Rock Slide Rampage.
- Inferno.
- Protection Move.
- Hard Skin.
as well as missing SFX added for a few moves.
- The joystick on mobile has been replaced with a new version that has been written directly into game code, resulting in more responsive motion, better performance, and less crashes.
- Bug fixed related to Bottle Caps quest.
- Bug fixed related to Spectators mode.
- Improved flow for in-game payments, allowing future features such as credit card/debit card payments to exist.

## Known Bugs

- Certain monster breeds might not be available as followers.
- Guild User interfaces might be buggy.
