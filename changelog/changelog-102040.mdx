A minor update focusing on Translations AI, Open APIs, and major performance updates.

## New Updates
- Season 1 claim has ended, with 59% of the Season 1 airdrop being spent + unclaimed. Unclaimed apartment and monster rewards will be redistributed among creators next in Wheel of Fortune V1 leaderboards.
- Translations System is live, with support for English, Spanish and Chinese. The game will be gradually translated and tested using AI and community suggestions.
- PvP Leagues Era 8 is live.
- Stellar Pass: Easter Eggs is live.
- 3 new mutations added: Schoktopus, Nikidna and Shrumel
- 1 new monster + 1 new variant added
- Storage Box: Added support for favourites, search, custom amount transfers and improved filters.
- Open APIs are live, developers can read more here: [https://help.spellborne.gg/community/openapi](https://help.spellborne.gg/community/openapi)
- Multiple bug fixes for task board, notifications, dialogue UI and more.
- Battle System: Includes improved dialogue speed, custom crystal animations, with the following fixes:
- Dark Waves (Audio fixed)
- Strategic Sleep (Special case fixed)
- Sail Protection (Bug fix)
- Blizzard Sprinter (Bug fix)
- Flame Nullifier (Bug fix)
- Massive speed improvements for map to map switch, vastly decreasing the time required to move from one map to another.
