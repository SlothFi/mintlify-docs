A minor update focusing on the transition towards Season 2, new content, and bug fixes.

## New Updates
- **PvP Leagues Test Phase**: Starts today. More information below.
- 4 new Monster Mutations: Coptabug, Ignipuff, Kaiju and Mujira unlock new mutations!
- **Tower of Eternity**: 3 new levels with more rewards, more challenging and exciting enemies added!
- Nearby finder is now more accessible, just click on another player to find more information about them!
- Payment issues related to bundles should be significantly lower.
- Color coding of certain monster sprites in battles has been improved.
- Changelog is now visible within the game for all players.

## Known Bugs
- League Battles across different servers might be buggy.
- ELO calculation might be a bit buggy, expect fixes later this week.
- Certain monster abilities might not work correctly.
- Certain monster breeds might not be available as followers.

## Ends Soon
- Spooked Copybara variant is available until 30th November 2024.

## Coming Next
- Season 2 starts on 25th November 2024 (details below).
- Mondo Swamp (*New location*) goes live on 29th November 2024.
- Achievements Systems are targeted to go live by end of November 2024.
- Improved Chat System is targeted to go live by end of November 2024.
- Professions are targeted to go live around mid December 2024.
- Settlers Glory: Apartment Staking, targeted to go live in early December, 2024.
- Guilds: Base Systems are under development, targeted to go live in early December, 2024.

## Notes

### Celebrating 20 Million Battles!

We're excited to share that Spellborne has crossed **20 million battles** in the wild and friendly PVP modes, with more than **2.1 million monsters captured**.

This feat could not have been achieved without the support of our active community, and the whole Mon Studios team is thankful for your support.

Please find a small token of appreciation in your mailbox sometime this week.

### PvP Leagues Test Phase is here!

Players can now access the test phase of PvP leagues until 24th November 2024 23:59 GMT, and share feedback regarding their experience in the Leagues battle system.

Any players participating (subject to multi account restrictions) in the test phase will be eligible for a limited edition woodot (2 innate stats with 50+ guaranteed values, gender of your choice).
