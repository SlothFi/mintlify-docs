A minor update focusing on the transition towards Season 2, new content, and bug fixes.

## New Updates
- **Season 2** is now live, read more below.
- **PvP Leagues Era 1** is now live, read more below.
- Chat system improvements are now live, with added support for commands such as /global, /local and /whisper.
- Achievements are now live in early access, claimable rewards to be added next month.
- Bundles can now be gifted to other players.
- Fixed bugs related to multiple moves and abilities.
- New skins added: Lucky Sea (SOL), SpellBR (MATIC) and Sir. Aldric (MATIC).

## Known Bugs
- Certain monster abilities might not work correctly.
- Certain monster breeds might not be available as followers.
- Social Stats system is currently going under migration, this may cause Social points from Season 1 to not appear.

## Ends Soon
- Spooked Copybara variant is available until 30th November 2024.

## Coming Next
- Stellar Pass: 31 days of Christmas goes live on 1st December 2024.

- Mobile Builds (iOS and Android) are near completion with target release date being around December 2024.
- Professions are targeted to go live around mid December 2024.
- Task Board upgrades are targeted to go live around mid December 2024.
- Settlers Glory: Apartment Staking, targeted to go live in early December, 2024.

## Notes

### Season 2: Risk and Reward is here!

The second season of Spellborne is live, starting today.

This season will focus on gradually introducing major feature updates such as Achievements, PvP Leagues, Professions, Mobile Builds and Guilds.

A total of 5% of the BORNE token supply has been kept for Season 2 rewards, in-addition to exciting rewards including 5 Large Apartments and 3 enchanted monsters.

Read More: [https://www.monstudios.co/blog/season-2](https://www.monstudios.co/blog/season-2)

### PvP Leagues Era 1 is here!

The first era of PvP leagues is live, starting with Badged (Ranked mode) and Randoms (Casual mode) leagues.

Players can battle against other players to gain ELO. Players can claim rare rewards including breeding anklets and innate boosted Copybara based on their ELO. These rewards are expected to go live later this week.

Players will be rewarded gameplay points towards the end of Season 2 based on their Era 1 ELO scores as well as Win Streaks.

PvP Leagues Era 1 is live until 10th January 2025.

Read more: [https://www.monstudios.co/blog/pvp-leagues](https://www.monstudios.co/blog/pvp-leagues)
