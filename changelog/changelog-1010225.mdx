A minor update focusing on PvP Leagues Era 3, bug fixes, new moves and improvements overall.

## New Updates
- **Stellar Pass**: The Pyramid Texts is here.
- **PvP Leagues Era 3** is live, with increased level cap of 37. Will be active until 14th Feb 2025.
- **Beacon Orbs event** is live until 11th Feb 2025, collect blue orbs by participating in battles, spend them in a 2048 minigame, to win golden orbs.
- Players can now hide their online status, in preferences.
- **Battle System**: Fixed HP/SP glitch in user interface. Players can now control monsters upto level 37 (Requires Perception set to 3, which is achieved by completing quests).
- **Daycare**: Improved the "Free monster into wild" flow, to reduce accidental clicks.
- User interface improvements to increase consistency + decrease crashes on iOS build.
- Added follower sprites for 3 monsters.
- Added support for creator codes and twitch drops.
