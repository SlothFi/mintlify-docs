A minor update focusing on staking, monster mounts, and battle pass.

- Monster Mutations added: Toxicondor (Ribbon Bow), Spacekong (FUD)
- Profile UI: Player profile user interface has been updated to support background animations, achievements, monster mastery, as well as provide the player with clearer information about skills, achievements, trading and creator material.

- Harvest of Nguzo: Stellar pass is here, with exciting new skins, items and rewards. Available till 23rd December, 2025.
- Bug fixes and reworks for Task Board, PvP ELO and monster abilities.
