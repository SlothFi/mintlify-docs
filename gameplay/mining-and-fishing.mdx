## Mining

Mining yields ores, gems, and minerals essential for crafting crystals and fertilisers. Players discover mining nodes in caves, mountains, and other world locations using pickaxes to extract both common and rare materials valued for crafting and commerce.

## Fishing

Fishing is a calm, skill-based activity for harvesting aquatic resources and consumables, available at bodies of water throughout the game world.

### Key Mechanics

- Catches range from standard fish to uncommon aquatic creatures, with unsuccessful attempts yielding unwanted items
- Features a mini-game based on timing and precision — simple to start, with room for mastery
- Species availability depends on time of day and weather conditions

Both systems support Spellborne's crafting economy and monster care systems while encouraging player exploration and interaction across different regions.
