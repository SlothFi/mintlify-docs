## Farming

Farming is central to resource generation, enabling players to grow crops for monster food, crafting ingredients, or marketplace trading.

- Unlocked early via Heiwa Village
- Plots support different seasonal crops and multi-season harvests
- Crops respond to economic supply and demand dynamics

### Growth Mechanics

- Crops operate on specific growth cycles with harvest windows (approximately 18 hours)
- Unharvested crops wither but convert to compost for other uses
- Different monsters have crop preferences that boost trust when satisfied

Farming directly influences monster happiness, battle performance, and player economic participation through seasonal engagement.

## Crafting

Crafting transforms raw materials into consumables and tools that enhance monster capabilities and enable player progression.

### How It Works

- Recipes acquired through gameplay progression, quests, or NPC interactions
- Combines crops, minerals, and monster drops as ingredients
- Produces four item categories:

| Category | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Healing Potions** | Restore HP or stamina |
| **Stat Boosters** | Temporarily increase combat stats |
| **Capture Enhancers** | Improve capture success rates |
| **Utility Items** | Various gameplay aids |

Crafting is essential for battle survival, enables economic specialization through trading, and creates synergies across farming, mining, and fishing systems.
