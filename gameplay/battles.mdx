Battles are the **heart of Spellborne**, merging strategic decision-making with monster growth and player choice.

## Overview

Turn-based combat where participants select from attacks, items, or switches each round. Success hinges on tactical planning and comprehending opponent strategies. Players may capture wild creatures mid-engagement to expand their roster.

## Core Actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Attack** | Deploy learned monster abilities |
| **Item** | Use consumables for healing or stat boosts |
| **Switch** | Exchange active monsters within your team |
| **Capture** | Employ crystals to trap wild monsters |

## Stats

Monster performance depends on seven core stats:

- **HP** — Health Points
- **Stamina** — Ability usage resource
- **Attack** — Physical damage output
- **Defense** — Physical damage reduction
- **Special Attack** — Special damage output
- **Special Defense** — Special damage reduction
- **Speed** — Turn order priority

<Note>
High-trust monsters perform better and respond dependably, while distrustful creatures may underperform or act unpredictably.
</Note>

## Battle Types

- **PvE** — Combat against environmental enemies and scripted opponents
- **PvP** — Player-versus-player confrontations testing team building and statistical mastery
- **Boss Encounters** — Larger, narrative-driven fights demanding preparation and resource management
