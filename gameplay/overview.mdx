The game combines monster collection, nurturing, and player-driven exploration with systems rewarding both casual and dedicated players. Core pillars are choice, trust, and ownership.

## Key Gameplay Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Monster Collecting & Training" icon="paw">
    Capture monsters with distinct traits and evolution options, building relationships through interaction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Battles & Strategy" icon="swords">
    Turn-based combat against NPCs and other players using tactical decisions and items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Resource Activities" icon="pickaxe">
    Farming, crafting, mining, and fishing mechanics that encourage strategic planning.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quests & Exploration" icon="map">
    Story-driven quests with meaningful choices across various environments and NPCs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Player-Driven Economy" icon="store">
    An open marketplace where player activity shapes prices and availability, creating a living in-game economy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trust & Progression" icon="heart">
    Advancement depends on both battle success and how players care for their companions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
