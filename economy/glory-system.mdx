The Glory System integrates apartments as stakeable NFTs, enabling players to earn Glory Points through engagement and investment.

## Apartments

- **Max Supply:** 3,333
- Available in Small, Medium, Large, and Penthouses
- Player-owned NFTs serving as social hubs and utility assets
- Support for cosmetic upgrades and customization

## Staking

Players generate Glory Points by staking apartment NFTs over time. The earning structure considers staking duration and seasonal multipliers. Players can also stake BORNE tokens to earn Glory Points.

## Glory Points

Glory Points are a non-tradable currency dedicated to progression and cosmetics, distinguishing them from standard tradeable assets.

## Rewards

Glory Points can be redeemed for:

- **Seasonal Items** — Limited edition items and rare event gear
- **Visual Enhancements** — Skins and furniture
- **Consumables** — Special-use items providing gameplay advantages
