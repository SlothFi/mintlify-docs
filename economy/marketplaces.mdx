Marketplaces are the **heartbeat of Spellborne's player economy**, enabling free trade of monsters, items, skins, and resources.

## Open Market Trading

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Monsters** | Capture, mint, and trade monsters as NFTs. Each monster is unique, with stats, personalities, and trust levels that affect value. |
| **Items & Consumables** | Healing items, crafting materials, and farming crops can be freely exchanged. |
| **Cosmetics & Skins** | Player-driven markets include visual customizations, often seasonal or limited-edition. |
| **Tools & Resources** | Crafting tools, ores, and fish can be bought or sold, giving gatherers and crafters ways to specialize. |

## Kanta Trader & Guild Markets

Kanta the Trader operates on multiple battle routes, offering guild-controlled market hubs.

- Guilds compete through bidding to operate these marketplaces
- Guilds can set discounts, limited-time bundles, or exclusive drops and sell cosmetic items

### Fee Structure

| Market Type | System Fee | Guild Revenue |
|------------|-----------|---------------|
| Main Markets | 5% | — |
| Guild Markets | 3% | Set by guild |
