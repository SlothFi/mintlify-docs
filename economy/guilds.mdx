Guilds function as economic and strategic entities with governance systems, revenue models, and competitive warfare mechanics centered on Guild Tokens.

## Guild Tokens

Tokens are minted using $BORNE, creating demand for the game's primary asset. Each guild determines its own initial token supply and distribution. Guild members, allies, and external parties can acquire tokens.

### Token Utility

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Speculation" icon="chart-line">
    External players purchase tokens to speculate on guild performance in wars and seasonal events. Values fluctuate based on victories and treasury growth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Governance" icon="scale-balanced">
    Token holders vote on marketplace fee structures, treasury spending, war tactics, and alliance formation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Economics" icon="coins">
    Tokens can be staked to generate returns linked to guild performance. Revenue streams are redistributed to holders.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Guild Wars

Guild Wars are seasonal competitions where groups battle for control, prestige, and economic dominance.

**Rewards:**
- $BORNE payments directed to guild treasuries
- Heightened token demand through increased speculative value

**Participant Roles:**
- Guild members engage directly in battles and resource gathering
- Speculators support guilds through token purchases, wagering on outcomes
