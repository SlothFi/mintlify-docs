Monsters are central to the Spellborne economy, obtainable through catching in the wild using crystals or collecting from events like Soul Exchange and Treasures of Celestia.

## Why Players Trade

- Breeding superior specimens
- Competing in PvP battles
- Completing collections
- Mastering different breed types

## NFT Minting

In order to trade monsters, a monster needs to be minted into a non-fungible token. This verification process enables marketplace transactions both within and outside the game.

## Minting Fee Controls

The system implements variable fees (ranging from $1 to $200 USD minimum) to prevent marketplace flooding and bot activity. Fees depend on:

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Account Age** | Established accounts receive lower fees; newer accounts face higher costs |
| **Breed Supply** | Popular breeds cost more to mint; rare breeds cost less |
| **Account Mints** | Rapid minting attempts trigger increased fees |

Additional undisclosed factors also influence pricing.

<Warning>
Players can mint on preferred blockchains, but directly transferring NFTs to unsupported wallets may result in them being displaced. Use the in-game marketplace for cross-chain transfers.
</Warning>
