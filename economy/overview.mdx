The Spellborne economy operates as a living, player-driven ecosystem focused on monsters, resources, guilds, and tokens. Every system feeds into another, generating continuous demand and meaningful progression.

## Foundation

- **Monsters** serve as the foundational asset — they can be captured, bred, traded, and converted to NFTs
- **BORNE token** functions as the economic backbone, earned through gameplay and spent across various systems
- Player actions directly influence scarcity and value distribution

## Revenue & Gameplay Fund

The Gameplay Fund replenishes through chest speedups, marketplace fees, and minting fees, then funds PvP rewards, guild events, tournaments, and liquidity incentives.

## Earning BORNE

- Monster and item trading
- Seasonal leaderboard competitions
- Guild warfare and territory control
- PvP league participation

## BORNE Utility

- NFT minting and monster trading
- Stellar Pass and progression purchases
- Marketplace transactions
- System speed-ups
- Guild token creation and DAO governance
- Future integration with other Mon Studios titles
