Mythril is a reward pool funded by PvP Chest speed-ups, incentivizing competitive play through token and cosmetic prizes.

## Pool Accumulation

When players expedite PvP Chests using BORNE tokens, 90% of the BORNE spent is added to the Mythril pool.

## Winning Mechanics

Daily winners receive up to **2,000 BORNE** from the accumulated pool. Win probability depends on:

- BORNE spent on individual chest speed-ups
- Player Reputation (currently in development)

The maximum probability cap is **5%**, achieved at 144 BORNE expenditure per speed-up.

## Rewards

| Reward | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **BORNE Tokens** | 2,000 BORNE per win |
| **Mythril Skin** | Permanent, untradeable cosmetic with +30% Training EXP bonus |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Candidate Identification">Identify eligible players</Step>

  <Step title="RNG Evaluation">Random number generation check</Step>
  <Step title="Pool Validation">Confirm minimum 2,000 BORNE in pool</Step>
  <Step title="Cooldown Check">48-hour restriction between wins</Step>
  <Step title="Payout">Execute payout with global announcement</Step>
</Steps>

## Future Development

The roadmap includes more frequent winners and multiple daily draws to further incentivize PvP activity.
