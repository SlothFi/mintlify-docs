## What is Spellborne?

Spellborne is a retro-styled monster collector RPG that merges nostalgic gameplay elements and modern technology, drawing inspiration from Pokemon (battling and collecting), Tamagotchi (care mechanics), and Stardew Valley (farming and crafting).

The ecosystem emphasizes player-controlled markets for monsters, resources, and cosmetics.

### Key Stats

- **Developer:** Mon Studios (indie studio)
- **Signups:** 175,000+
- **Battles:** 32 million
- **Monsters Captured:** 4 million
- **Franchise:** First title in an expanding IP (Guardians of Celestia and Spellborne Rogue in development)
