The Spellborne Creators Program (#SpellborneCreate) supports content creators through in-game rewards, content creation support, community recognition and financial incentives.

## Apply

The program undergoes periodic updates, and manual application review may take time for approval.

## Benefits

### Stellar Pass Access
Active participants receive complimentary premium membership and gifted subscriptions to distribute among followers.

### Custom Cosmetics
Approved creators unlock branded in-game skins and exclusive profile pictures. Guild leaders managing communities exceeding 100 members receive discounted custom guild cosmetic options.

### Monetization
The program provides Twitch Drops functionality and personalized creator codes. Members earn **5% of the purchases made using their creator code**, with payouts distributed monthly once earnings reach $100 USD minimum.

### Community Recognition
Participants gain an exclusive Discord role and distinctive in-game profile borders.

## Disclosure Requirements

Creators must conspicuously disclose relationships to the company. Acceptable language includes:

- `#Ad`
- `#Sponsored`
- `#SpellborneCreate`
- "In connection with Mon Studios Creators Program, I may receive payouts from your in-game purchases and actions."

<Warning>
Clear disclosure is the program's highest priority requirement.
</Warning>
