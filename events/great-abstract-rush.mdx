The Great Abstract Rush is a **limited-time event** designed to promote trading activity in the Bazaar marketplace and encourage community growth through referrals. Players earn **Rush Points** by trading items and can spend these points on exclusive mystery boxes.

## Event Duration

| | Date |
|---|---|
| **Start** | March 25, 2026 00:00 UTC |
| **End** | April 25, 2026 23:59 UTC |

## How to Earn Rush Points

### Trading in the Bazaar

Players earn Rush Points for every trade completed in the Bazaar.

| Action | Points Earned |
|--------|--------------|
| Buy a listing | 1 point per 10 BORNE spent |
| Complete an offer | 1 point per 10 BORNE spent |

<Note>
A minimum of 10 BORNE must be spent per trade to earn Rush Points. Points are awarded to the buyer (spender), not the seller.
</Note>

### Referring Players

Invite friends to play Spellborne and earn ongoing rewards from their trading activity.

| Reward | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Referral Bonus** | Earn 10% of all Rush Points your referrals generate through trading |
| **How it works** | Your referral trades in the Bazaar, they get full points, you get a 10% bonus on top |

<Info>
Referral bonuses are awarded automatically. Share your referral code from the Social tab in your player profile.
</Info>

### Example

> A referred player spends 1,000 BORNE in the Bazaar.
> - **They earn:** 100 Rush Points (1,000 / 10)
> - **You earn:** 10 Rush Points (10% bonus)

## How to Spend Rush Points

### Mystery Box

Spend your Rush Points to open mystery boxes containing exclusive rewards.

| | Details |
|---|---|
| **Cost** | 1,000 Rush Points |
| **Contents** | Rare cosmetics and abstract shards |
| **Weighting** | Items you don't already own have higher drop rates |

<Note>
Mystery boxes can be opened from the Rush Points popup by clicking the Rush Points counter in the top-right of the HUD.
</Note>

## Where to Find It

- **Rush Points Counter** — displayed in the top-right HUD during the event period
- **Rush Points Popup** — click the counter to see your balance, earn methods, and open mystery boxes
- **Referral Code** — found in the Social tab of your player profile

## Anti-Abuse

- Only completed trades count toward Rush Points
- Cancelled trades, expired listings, and failed transactions do not award points
- Self-trading or wash trading may result in point deduction and account action

    Rush Points will remain on your account. Future events may reuse them.
  </Accordion>
